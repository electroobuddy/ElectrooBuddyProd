## 🚨 Common Mistakes to Avoid

### Don't:
- ❌ Change your business name/format on different platforms
- ❌ Buy fake reviews (Google WILL catch you)
- ❌ Ignore negative reviews
- ❌ Expect overnight results
- ❌ Stuff keywords unnaturally
- ❌ Copy content from other websites

### Do:
- ✅ Keep NAP (Name, Address, Phone) consistent everywhere
- ✅ Respond professionally to ALL reviews
- ✅ Post fresh content regularly
- ✅ Focus on customer experience
- ✅ Be patient and persistent
- ✅ Track your progress monthly
