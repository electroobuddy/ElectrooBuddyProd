### 2. Conversational Content Optimization

AI assistants process natural language queries. Optimize for conversational searches:

#### Target Question-Based Queries

Create content that answers these specific questions:

**Price/Cost Questions:**
```
Q: "How much does an electrician charge in Ujjain?"
A: Create detailed pricing guide with ranges:
   - Basic service call: ₹300-500
   - Hourly rates: ₹400-800/hour
   - Common repairs with price ranges
   - Emergency service premiums
```

**Recommendation Questions:**
```
Q: "Who is the best electrician near me?"
A: Create content highlighting:
   - Customer ratings and reviews
   - Years in business
   - Certifications and qualifications
   - Service guarantees
   - Response times
```

**How-To Questions:**
```
Q: "How do I fix a flickering light?"
A: Create step-by-step guides:
   1. Safety first (turn off power)
   2. Check the bulb
   3. Inspect the switch
   4. When to call a professional
   5. Warning signs requiring expert help
```

**Comparison Questions:**
```
Q: "Should I repair or replace my electrical panel?"
A: Create comparison content:
   - Cost comparison table
   - Age considerations
   - Safety factors
   - Long-term value
   - Professional recommendation criteria
```

### 3. Featured Snippet Optimization

AI often pulls from Google's featured snippets. Structure content for snippet selection:

#### Paragraph Snippets (40-60 words)
```html
<h2>What Services Does Electroo Buddy Provide?</h2>
<p>Electroo Buddy provides comprehensive electrical services including residential wiring, 
commercial installations, industrial maintenance, emergency repairs, and electrical product 
sales. Our certified electricians serve Ujjain and surrounding areas with 24/7 emergency 
support and transparent pricing.</p>
```

#### List Snippets
```html
<h3>Signs You Need an Emergency Electrician:</h3>
<ol>
  <li>Burning smell from outlets or switches</li>
  <li>Sparks when using electrical devices</li>
  <li>Frequent circuit breaker trips</li>
  <li>Flickering or dimming lights</li>
  <li>Buzzing sounds from electrical panel</li>
  <li>Warm or discolored outlets</li>
</ol>
```

#### Table Snippets
```html
<table>
  <thead>
    <tr>
      <th>Service Type</th>

    </tr>

      <td>Fan Installation</td>
      <td>₹300-500</td>
      <td>30-60 minutes</td>
    </tr>
    <!-- More rows -->
  </tbody>
</table>
```
