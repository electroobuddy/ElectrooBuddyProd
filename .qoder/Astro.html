Frequently Asked Questions

❓
For an accurate Kundli, you need your exact date of birth, time of birth (as precise as possible), and place of birth. Even a rough time helps — if you are unsure, Panditji can use birth rectification techniques to identify the probable time based on key life events.
Yes! We offer consultations via phone call, WhatsApp video call, and Google Meet for clients across India and internationally. Written kundli reports with detailed analysis can also be emailed to you within 48 hours of booking.
A standard Kundli consultation lasts 45–60 minutes. A detailed life reading (covering all aspects — career, marriage, health, finances) may take 90 minutes. Quick question-focused sessions of 30 minutes are also available.
Vedic astrology identifies planetary periods (dashas), transits, and yogas that indicate trends, opportunities and challenges in specific timeframes. It is a powerful guidance tool — not a crystal ball. Free will and karma both play a role. Panditji will always recommend constructive remedies alongside predictions.
Kundli Milan (Ashtakoot matching) is the Vedic compatibility analysis of two individuals' birth charts before marriage. It examines 8 factors including Varna, Vashya, Tara, Yoni, Graha Maitri, Gana, Bhakut and Nadi, scoring a total of 36 points. A score of 18+ is generally considered acceptable. While not mandatory, it helps couples understand compatibility and potential challenges.
For smaller poojas (Satyanarayan Katha, Namkaran), 3–5 days advance notice is usually sufficient. For larger ceremonies (Grah Pravesh, Navgraha Havan, Rudrabhishek), we recommend booking at least 1–2 weeks ahead to ensure proper preparation and availability of all pooja materials.
Remedies are optional but highly recommended when specific planetary afflictions are identified. They may include mantras, specific poojas, dietary changes, charity, or wearing gemstones. Gemstone recommendations are made very carefully, considering the full chart — wearing the wrong stone can sometimes be counterproductive. Panditji will never recommend unnecessary remedies.
